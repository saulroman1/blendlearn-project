Welcome to BlendLearn!

The hub of finding help on how to use blender!


In this hub, you will find plenty of tutorials that are based on things you would need at your blender level. From first opening up blender and seeing a cube. To being able to sculpt or model and adding realistic/cartoon materials!

Blender is used for a variety of things, from animation, art work, architecture, or video editing, Blender has a lot of uses! If you are first starting out in blender, I suggest to click here!

If you are more familiar with blender and understand some shortcuts, go to the intermediate page in order to work on some projects and further progress your skills!

If you are more advanced in blender and know all the shortcuts etc, go to the advanced page in order to learn character rigging, animation, how to use nodes properly to make perfect shaders, and more!
